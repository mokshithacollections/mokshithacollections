Care Instructions for Silk Sarees

Do's
  • Always dry clean your silk saree
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Wrap in muslin cloth or cotton for storage
  • Use padded hangers for storage if needed
  • Iron on low heat with a cloth barrier
Don'ts
  • Never machine wash or soak in water
  • Avoid contact with perfumes and deodorants
  • Don't store in plastic covers for long periods
  • Avoid direct heat while ironing
  • Don't hang with metal clips
Pro Tip: To maintain the zari work, sprinkle some talcum powder on the zari portions before storing to prevent tarnishing.
